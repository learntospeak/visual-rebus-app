## End-of-session questions

1. Which puzzle produced the best “aha” moment?
2. Which answer felt arbitrary or unfamiliar?
3. Did any clue waste information or reveal too much?
4. Did the explanation make every visual element clear?
5. Was any screen cluttered or difficult to read?
6. Would you play a daily puzzle tomorrow?
7. Would you share a puzzle challenge with someone?
