# Store Listing Brief

## Positioning sentence

Quick visual riddles that feel clever, never cheap — useful clues, clear explanations and a new challenge every day.

## Store keywords to test

- visual riddles
- rebus puzzles
- picture riddles
- brain teasers
- visual word game
- word puzzles
- lateral thinking
- daily puzzle

## Screenshot story

1. Challenge: “Can you solve this visual phrase in 10 seconds?”
2. Help: “Clues guide you without spoiling it.”
3. Reward: “See exactly why the answer works.”
4. Habit: “One fresh daily challenge.”
5. Variety: “Words, pictures, motion and interaction.”

## Description promises that must remain true

- Ads and ad-free purchase wording are unambiguous.
- Offline availability is accurately stated.
- Puzzle count reflects approved playable puzzles only.
- No unsupported “best” or “number one” claims.
- Regional packs are labelled.
